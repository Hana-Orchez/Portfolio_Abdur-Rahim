Diasporic Divas

Diasporic Divas is a timeline of Black Femme Musicians across the African Diaspora dated from the 1930-2020s. Diasporic Divas is similar to a jukebox, each alpha-numerical key coincides with the first letter of the artist or the era they are from. 'Diasporic Divas' showcases art,history and technology on one platform.
